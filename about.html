Task Management Application

Welcome to our Task Management Application, your ultimate tool for organizing and prioritizing tasks with ease.

Mission:

Our mission is to empower individuals to take control of their time and productivity by providing a user-friendly platform that simplifies task management.

Features:

Intuitive Interface: Enjoy an easy-to-navigate interface designed for a seamless user experience. Customizable Tasks: Create and manage tasks with customizable titles, priorities, statuses, and due dates to fit your unique workflow. Local Storage: Your tasks are securely saved in your browser's local storage, giving you access anytime, anywhere. Task Filtering: Quickly sort and filter tasks based on their status to stay organized and focused. Notifications: Receive timely notifications to remind you of upcoming tasks and deadlines.
